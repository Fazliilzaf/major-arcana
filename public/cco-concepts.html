CCO visual directions

Three still-image concepts

Same CCO functions, three different interface directions. These are concept stills for alignment, not implementation proofs.

Concept 2B

Dialogue First + more mail client

Same core recommendation, but pushed harder toward a real mail product. The inbox speaks in sender, subject, preview and timing. The right side becomes a conversation-and-compose window, not a workflow form.

Concept 2

Dialogue First

Recommended. A single communication workspace with a compact command dock, a living inbox stream, and a customer dialogue surface that behaves like an actual email tool.

Concept 1

The Stream

Closest to a premium inbox app. Mail stream dominates, while the active thread slides in as a lighter response layer.

Concept 3

Journey Timeline

Best for customer history and multi-thread clarity. The central story is the relationship, not just the latest email.
